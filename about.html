About Nandom Senior High School

Nandom Senior High School is a second cycle institution located in Nandom in the Upper West Region of Ghana. It was established in 1968 as a community day school by the Catholic Diocese of Wa. The school has since grown to become one of the prominent senior high schools in the region, known for its academic excellence and holistic development of students.

The school offers a variety of academic programs, including General Arts, General Science, Business, Visual Arts, and Agricultural Science.

Nandom Senior High School has a rich history of achievements in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. The school emphasizes discipline, hard work, and community service, preparing students to become responsible citizens and leaders in their communities.
