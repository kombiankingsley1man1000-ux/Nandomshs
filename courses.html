General Secience Department

Focuses on subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Elective Mathematics. Prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, natural sciences, and technology. Supported by well-equipped science laboratories for practical learning.

General Arts Department

Includes subjects such as Literature, History, Geography, and Economics. Ideal for students interested in humanities, social sciences, and creative fields and also aims to develop critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills.. Encourages critical analysis, research skills, and effective communication. Ideal for students aiming for careers in law, social sciences, education, and public administration

Business Department

Covers subjects like Accounting, Business Management, Economics, and Marketing. Prepares students for careers in commerce, finance, entrepreneurship, and management. Emphasizes practical skills in accounting, business operations, and economic principles and equip students with knowledge in finance, entrepreneurship, and organizational management. Prepares graduates for careers in commerce, banking, and business administration
